Hire a ninja cat

A ninja cat (Ninjus Gattus) is an animal belonging to feline family. They can be found wandering at night in parking lots or in the countryside searching for other ninja cats to fight.

The main purpose of a ninja cat is to scare the shit out of other ninja cats. Once this goal is achieved, the ninja cat searches for a food source near to crowded places, so that other ninja cats won't be able to catch him unprepared and scare the shit out of him too. (Fig 1)
