Hire a Ninja Cat



A ninja cat (Ninjus Gattus) is an animal belonging to feline family. They can be found wandering at night in parking lots or in the countryside searching for other ninja cats to fight.

The main purpose of a ninja cat is to scare the shit out of other ninja cats. Once this goal is achieved, the ninja cat searches for a food source near to crowded places, so that other ninja cats won't be able to catch him unprepared and scare the shit out of him too.

The most important thing to remember about ninja cats is that they always have a ninja dude cat, which always rushes into helping his/her own ninja dude cat fighting against infedels who don't believe in ninja cats or any other enemy. Every ninja cat can only have 1 ninja dude cat and they remain dudes for the rest of their ninja lives.
